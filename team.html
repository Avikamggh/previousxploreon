Avikam Deol

Founder & CEO Xploreon

Avikam Deol

As the Founder and CEO of Xploreon, he leads the company’s vision to push the boundaries of space exploration and deep-tech innovation. With a passion for astrophysics, engineering, and AI, he began building Xploreon to make advanced science more accessible and impactful. Despite being a young founder, he has already led the development of simulations, educational tools, and early-stage R&D projects under the brand. His hands-on approach, from designing technology to building community, reflects a bold and future-forward mindset. Under his leadership, Xploreon is emerging as a platform where big ideas meet real execution—shaping the next generation of space-tech from the ground up.
